A Delicious Cheesecake

This dish is a timeless classic that I'll never feel guilty about eating. There is something about perfectly golden crust and the strawberry topping that makes this dessert flawless. I am always craving cheesecake and my boyfriend is always debating buying it in stores,so it is high time where I take the time to make it myself. Now I am not a baker at any means, so I was really looking for a recipes that would let me follow relatively easily.

Look at it! It looks so simple but so delicious!!!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup margarine
  • 3 (8 oz) packages cream cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups sour cream

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). In a medium bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs, 3 tablespoons sugar, cinnamon and melted margarine. Mix all ingredients well and press into bottom and sides of a 9 inch springform pan.
  2. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Blend in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla and salt. Fold in the sour cream.
  3. Pour batter into springform pan. Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour. Turn off oven and leave cake in for at least 30 minutes.
